Food as a Passion

Growing up my grandparents were in the hospitality industry and I learned from a young age that if your going to do something, don't do it halfway. I also learned that making food from fresh ingredients is key to a successful establishment. I lived in Portland, Oregon for a few years during Portland's foodie heyday. A lot of the amazing restaurants fell victim to the pandemic, sadly. This inspired me to experiment in the kitchen and have had a lot of fun trying to recreate some of my favorite dishes.
